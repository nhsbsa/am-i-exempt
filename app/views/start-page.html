Check and manage your exemption from paying NHS costs

Use this service to:

  • check if you have a current valid exemption
  • use it as proof (digital exemption), if asked by a pharmacist or dentist
  • check when it expires or whether it has expired recently
  • update your name, address or billing details with us
{#

What we can check

Because this service is new, we can currently tell you if you have a:

  • prescription prepayment certificate (PPC)
  • medical exemption certificate
  • maternity exemption certificate
  • tax credit exemption certificate
  • HC2 certificate
  • Passported benefit from DWP
#}

When you don't need to check

You're automatically entitled to free NHS prescriptions and NHS dental treatment if you are:

  • under 16
  • under 19 and in full time education
